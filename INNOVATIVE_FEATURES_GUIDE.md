## üöó 1. CARPOOL & RIDESHARE BOARD

### The Problem
- Students commute from Kansas City, Joplin, Springfield, Tulsa (1-3 hour drives)
- Gas is expensive ($40-80 per trip)
- Parking passes cost $100-300/semester
- Students drive alone, wasting money and increasing carbon footprint
- No easy way to find ride partners

### The Solution
**Gorilla-Link Rideshare** - A safe, campus-exclusive carpooling platform

### Key Features
- **Trip Types**: One-time trips or recurring schedules (M/W/F classes)
- **Smart Matching**: Filter by origin city, departure time, direction (to/from campus)
- **Cost Sharing**: Split gas money fairly (typically $10-15 per person)
- **Safety First**: PSU email verification, driver ratings, phone contact
- **Flexibility**: Last-minute rides, weekend trips home, event carpools
- **Popular Routes**: Kansas City, Joplin, Springfield, Tulsa, Wichita

### Impact
- **Save Money**: $40 gas trip ‚Üí $10 per person (4 riders)
- **Build Community**: Meet fellow students from your hometown
- **Reduce Emissions**: Fewer cars = greener campus
- **Weekend Trips**: Make going home for weekends affordable

### Routes (55+ route combinations)
`/rideshare` - Browse all rides
`/rideshare/offer` - Post your ride
`/rideshare/my-rides` - Manage your trips
`/rideshare/popular-routes` - See common routes

## üíö 3. MENTAL HEALTH & WELLNESS TRACKER

### The Problem
- College mental health crisis (40% of students report depression/anxiety)
- PSU Counseling Center is overwhelmed (2-week wait times)
- Students don't track stress patterns
- Don't know when to seek help
- Stigma around mental health conversations
- Crisis resources are scattered and hard to find

### The Solution
**Wellness Dashboard** - Private mood tracking + instant access to resources

### Key Features
- **Daily Check-Ins**: Track mood, stress, sleep, energy (1-10 scale)
- **Stress Factors**: Identify triggers (exams, finances, relationships)
- **Self-Care Tracking**: Log exercise, healthy eating, social activities
- **Trend Analysis**: See patterns over 7/30/90 days with charts
- **Smart Alerts**: "You've been stressed for 5+ days - Here are resources"
- **Resource Directory**: PSU Counseling, Crisis Hotline, Academic Help, Financial Aid
- **24/7 Crisis Resources**: National Suicide Hotline, Crisis Text Line prominently displayed
- **Privacy First**: Data is private, never shared, completely anonymous

### Impact
- **Early Intervention**: Catch mental health issues before crisis
- **Reduce Stigma**: Normalize tracking wellness like fitness
- **Better Self-Awareness**: Understand stress patterns (finals week, Mondays, etc.)
- **Quick Help**: One click to crisis resources instead of Googling in panic
- **Preventative Care**: Daily check-ins encourage self-reflection

### Crisis Resources Included
- **National Suicide Prevention**: 988
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741
- **PSU Counseling**: (620) 235-4522
- **Campus Police (Emergency)**: (620) 235-4624
- **SafeRide**: Campus transport when you don't feel safe

### Routes (7+ routes)

## üîë 4. LOST & FOUND BOARD

### The Problem
- Students lose wallets, keys, phones, laptops, AirPods constantly
- PSU Lost & Found is one tiny office (Overman Student Center)
- Hours are limited (M-F 8am-5pm)
- No way to search what's been found
- Items sit unclaimed for months
- No notification system

### The Solution
**Smart Lost & Found** - Campus-wide, searchable, 24/7 accessible

### Key Features
- **Two-Way Posts**: Report lost items AND found items
- **Smart Categories**: Phone, keys, wallet, laptop, clothing, IDs, jewelry, etc.
- **Detailed Search**: Filter by location, date, color, brand
- **Photo Uploads**: Picture of found items (blurred for security)
- **Location Mapping**: "Lost near Gorilla Crossing" or "Found in Axe Library"
- **Auto-Matching**: Get notified if description matches
- **Expiration**: Posts auto-expire after 60 days
- **Privacy Options**: Contact via Gorilla-Link message (no phone sharing)

### Impact
- **Find Lost Items**: 10x more likely to recover lost belongings
- **Good Samaritan Culture**: Easier to return found items
- **24/7 Access**: Check at midnight when you realize phone is missing
- **Reduce Anxiety**: Immediate action when you lose something important
- **Save Money**: Don't have to replace $200 textbook or $1000 laptop

### Common Lost Items at PSU
- Student IDs (building access!)
- Car/dorm keys
- AirPods/earbuds
- Wallets
- Textbooks
- Laptops
- Jackets/hoodies
- Water bottles
- Calculators

**Verified Sublease Board** - PSU-exclusive, safe, organized

- **Detailed Listings**: Photos, rent, utilities, amenities, roommate info
- **Flexible Terms**: Semester, summer, full year, specific months
- **Filter Search**: Price range, bedrooms, furnished, pets, parking
- **Neighborhood Info**: Distance to campus, walkability score
- **Roommate Compatibility**: Gender preference, lifestyle habits
- **Contact Protection**: Message through platform initially

### Typical PSU Rentals
- **Campus View Apartments**: $600-800/month, walking distance
- **Gorilla Heights**: $500-700/month, shuttle route
- **Downtown Houses**: $400-600/room, 5-10 min drive

### Impact
- **Save Thousands**: Don't pay double rent ($3000+ saved on summer sublet)
- **Graduate Stress-Free**: Find replacement tenant in days, not months
- **Study Abroad Confidence**: Know you can sublet your place
- **Better Matches**: Find roommates with similar lifestyle

## üë®‚Äçüè´ 7. PEER TUTORING MARKETPLACE

### The Problem
- PSU Tutoring Center has limited hours (M-Th 10am-8pm, F 10am-2pm)
- Only covers certain subjects (mostly math/science)
- Can't choose your tutor or schedule
- Long wait times during midterms/finals
- No tutoring for upper-level courses (CHEM 421, CS 470, etc.)
- Professional tutors cost $40-80/hour (unaffordable)

### The Solution
**Peer Tutoring Marketplace** - Student-to-student tutoring, affordable, flexible

### Key Features
- **Student Tutors**: Upperclassmen who aced the class tutor underclassmen
- **All Subjects**: Math, science, writing, business, nursing, tech, languages
- **Affordable Rates**: $10-25/hour (vs $50+ professional)
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Evening, weekends, online, in-person
- **Verified Tutors**: GPA verification, course completion proof
- **Rating System**: Students rate tutors after each session
- **First Session Free**: Many tutors offer free trial (15-30 min)
- **Group Discounts**: Split cost with study group ($5/person)

### How It Works
1. **Tutors Create Profile**: List subjects, rates, availability, experience
2. **Students Search**: Filter by subject, price, rating, availability
3. **Book Session**: Schedule time, location (library, Zoom, etc.)
4. **Meet & Learn**: 1-on-1 or small group tutoring
5. **Rate & Review**: Build tutor reputation

### Impact
- **Better Grades**: Personalized help when you need it
- **Make Money**: Smart students earn $15-25/hour tutoring
- **Flexible Learning**: Schedule around YOUR classes, not center hours
- **Peer Connection**: Learn from someone who just took the class
- **Confidence Boost**: One-on-one attention builds understanding

### Popular Subjects at PSU
- **STEM**: MATH 147, CHEM 121, PHYS 201, BIO 101
- **Business**: ACCT 201, ECON 202, FIN 301
- **Tech**: CS 101, CS 201, MIS 140
- **Writing**: ENGL 101, ENGL 102, research papers
- **Nursing**: MED 320, NUR 425, HESI prep

## üéÅ 8. FREE STUFF / GIVEAWAY BOARD

### The Problem
- Students throw away perfectly good stuff when moving out
- End-of-semester dumpsters are full of furniture, electronics, supplies
- Freshmen need mini-fridges, lamps, decorations but can't afford
- Seniors have everything but nowhere to donate
- Facebook Marketplace requires meeting strangers
- Goodwill is 20 minutes away, no one has a truck

### The Solution
**Free Stuff Board** - Campus-exclusive free item exchange

### Key Features
- **100% Free**: No selling, only giving away
- **Categories**: Furniture, electronics, clothes, textbooks, kitchen, decor
- **Condition Rating**: New, like-new, good, fair (be honest!)
- **Pickup Details**: Dorm/apartment location, availability
- **Photo Uploads**: See item before claiming
- **Student-Only**: PSU email required (safety)
- **First-Come First-Serve**: Quick claim button
- **Expiration**: Items auto-expire if not claimed in 14 days

### Common Free Items
- **Furniture**: Futons, desks, chairs, bookshelves, storage bins
- **Appliances**: Mini-fridges, microwaves, coffee makers
- **School Supplies**: Binders, calculators, backpacks
- **Textbooks**: Old editions still useful for studying
- **Decor**: Posters, lamps, rugs, curtains
- **Kitchen**: Dishes, utensils, pots, pans
- **Electronics**: Phone chargers, speakers, monitors
- **Clothes**: Professional clothes for interviews, winter coats

### When It's Most Active
- **End of Semester**: May/December (moving out)
- **Start of Semester**: August (clearing space)
- **Graduation**: May (getting rid of EVERYTHING)

### Impact
- **Save Money**: Furnish dorm for $0 instead of $500+
- **Reduce Waste**: 1000+ lbs of usable items saved from landfills
- **Help Community**: Seniors help freshmen, pay it forward
- **Sustainability**: Circular economy on campus
- **Move-Out Stress**: Easy way to get rid of stuff quickly

### Routes (5+ routes)
`/free-stuff` - Browse free items
`/free-stuff/post` - Give away item
