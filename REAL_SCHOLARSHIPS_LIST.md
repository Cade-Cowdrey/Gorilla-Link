## üéì TIPS FOR STUDENTS

### Apply Early!
- Don't wait until the deadline
- Some scholarships review applications on rolling basis

### Apply to Multiple
- Average student should apply to 10-20 scholarships
- More applications = better odds

### Read Requirements Carefully
- Each scholarship has specific eligibility
- Make sure you qualify before spending time

### Strong Essays Win
- Most scholarships require essays
- Get feedback from teachers/counselors
- Be authentic and tell your story

### Letters of Recommendation
- Ask teachers early (at least 2 weeks notice)
- Choose teachers who know you well
- Provide them with your resume/achievements
