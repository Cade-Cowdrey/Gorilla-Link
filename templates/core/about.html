About PittState-Connect

PittState-Connect is a digital bridge built by students, alumni, and faculty of Pittsburg State University — connecting Gorillas across generations through mentorship, networking, and shared opportunity.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower every Gorilla with meaningful professional connections and opportunities — ensuring that Pittsburg State’s legacy of excellence continues long after graduation. By leveraging technology and community, PittState-Connect transforms networking into an active partnership.

Our Vision

We envision a connected university ecosystem where students, alumni, and employers collaborate to grow careers, businesses, and the region. PittState-Connect acts as the central hub for mentorship, job matching, and event engagement — all driven by PSU pride.

Built With Pride

PittState-Connect is powered by innovation, fueled by Gorilla spirit, and designed to strengthen the bond between Pittsburg State University and its incredible community of students, alumni, and partners.
