About PittState-Connect

PittState-Connect bridges students, alumni, faculty, and the Pittsburg community. Created by Gorillas for Gorillas, it empowers mentorship, networking, and opportunity sharing across all departments of Pittsburg State University.

Our mission is to help every Gorilla find connections that matter — professionally, academically, and personally — while celebrating PSU’s legacy of excellence and community impact.
