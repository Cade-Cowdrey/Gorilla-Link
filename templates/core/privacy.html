Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how PittState-Connect collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, department, and affiliation with Pittsburg State University when you register or use PittState-Connect. Usage data like login activity, posts, and events you engage with may also be recorded to enhance your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to improve platform performance, connect you with relevant students and alumni, share opportunities, and maintain a safe, collaborative environment for all users.

3. Data Protection

All user data is stored securely and encrypted where appropriate. Access to data is limited to authorized staff and system administrators to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your information.

4. Your Rights

You may request data deletion or export at any time by contacting our support team. We are committed to maintaining transparency and control over your personal data.

5. Contact Information

For privacy-related concerns, reach out to us at privacy@pittstateconnect.com.
